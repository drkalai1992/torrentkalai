About The Conference

Objective of this International Level Interdisciplinary Conference is to facilitate academicians, researchers, industrialists & UG/PG students by presenting cutting edge research issues, Challenges and latest advances in the interdisciplinary field.

Call For Papers

This Conference invites unpublished research papers, articles and case studies from authors to present and exchange their insights on the recent developments in Engineering and Technology.

Topics to be covered includes (but not limited to):

  • o Ad-hoc and Sensor Networks
  • o Biomedical Engineering / Informatics
  • o Cloud and Mobile computing
  • o Digital Signal Processing
  • o Digital and Medical Image Processing
  • o Embedded Systems
  • o Remote Sensing
  • o RF, Microwaves and Smart Antennas
  • o Wireless and Optical Communication
  • o VLSI Design
  • o Machine Learning

Awards & Journal Publication

Few presentations during the technical session will be awarded as “Best Paper” based on technical session-chair recommendation.

All the accepted papers will be published in Conference proceedings and in SCOPUS/Web of Science (WoS) Indexed Journals with author’s concern with additional charges.

Paper Guidelines

Authors should restrict their papers to a maximum of 5 pages (Strictly in IEEE format). Submit your papers to icspcs2023@mkce.ac.in
